About us

We are About to Break – a five-piece metalcore band from the Mannheim area. With a vocalist who originally brings her sound from R’n’B, we blend heavy riffs, emotion, and catchiness in our own unique way. Since 2023, we’ve been working together on our sound – raw, honest, and full of energy. After an intense time in the studio, our debut EP -a secret to keep- is now ready to be released.
